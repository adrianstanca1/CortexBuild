### **Event Flow**
```
1. Click pe buton
   ↓
2. preventDefault() - oprește comportamentul default
   ↓
3. stopPropagation() - oprește event bubbling
   ↓
4. Find target section by ID
   ↓
5. Update active states
   ↓
6. Smooth scroll to section
   ↓
7. Log success message
```
